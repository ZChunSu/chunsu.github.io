Introduction

TGU-UOW Computer Vision Lab aims to provide applied research fundamental of vision computing principles to all aspects of applications in the domains of Computer-aided diagnosis (CAD), Computer-aided surgery (CAS) and Healthcare education support (HES). It integrates physics, geometry, image processing, and machine learning, into computerized techniques that assist radiologists in their medical decision-making processes.
